Longboard Raiser

As you probably know, Longboards has larger wheels than classic skates. To prevent the wheels from touching the wood, raisers are used between the axles and the board.
During last sumer, I bought a pretty old longboard in order to repair it and use it for short distance transportation in the city. Raisers where ruined because of the time and painted in horrible colors.

I though would be better made them by myself than just bought new ones. This is my 3D design of a classical longboard raiser. You can open this in Thingiverse or download it cliking here.

Bottle Water top
