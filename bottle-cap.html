A million plastic bottles are bought around the world every minute and only 8.4% of it eventually got recycled. Using a refillable bottle is a small but necessary gesture. I found a perfect bottle made in aluminum at home but the cap was not in good shape. Instead of buying a new one I just modeled and printed one.


3D Print


First, take a look on Thingiverse.
If you wish to 3D Print this model, I would recommend the following parameters:


- Cap -


  • Layer height: <0.2mm (recommended)
  • Infill: 40% (recommended)
  • Support: needed*
  • Material: PETG ()recommended

- Gasket -


  • Layer height: 0.2mm
  • Infill: 100%
  • Infill patron: Concentric
  • Material: TPU (or any flexible material)
